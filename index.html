About Nuru

Art is a vital part of what makes us human. We all depend on it for entertainment and for reflection on an individual and a global level. Despite that, it continues to be challenging for artists to make a living consistently. Nuru looks to help solve that issue by creating a platform for artists to find their audience and build and maintain networks online to help them find more consistent gigs and monetize their following more effectively.
